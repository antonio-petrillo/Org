#+title: Scrum
#+filetags: :agile:incremental:engineering:

* Can be considered an [[id:28c613f5-eb22-4793-bf9f-1acbe38606a2][Agile]] method
It is defined as a /framework/ for projects organization.
* Diagram
[[../assets/sw-scrum.png]]
* Jargons
| Term            | Definition                                                                           |
|-----------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Team            | team of developers (at most 7) that can operate autonomously                         |
| Increment       | Increment made after a /sprint/ (see later), potentially it could be released        |
| Product backlog | A series of requirements that the software should meet, since it's an agile method they can change |
| Product owner   | The final clients for our software                                                   |
| Scrum           | Daily meeting to analyze the progess made                                            |
| Scrum Master    | It takes care that the Scrum process is respected                                    |
| Sprint          | Cycle of develop that should produce an increment, usually last for 2 to 4 weeks     |
| Speed           | ETA for a feature to be implemented, its main purpouse is understand if a feature can fit in a sprint |
