* Properties of asymptotic growth symbols
** Transitivity
If $f(n) = O(g(n))$ and $g(n) = O(h(n))$ then $f(n) = O(h(n))$.
*** Proof
Pick $c = max(c_{g(n)}, c_{h(n)})$ and $n_0 = \max(n_{0_{g(n)}}, n_{0_{h(n)}})$
$$\blacksquare$$
*** Note
This apply also to $\Omega$ and $\Theta$, the proof are similar.
